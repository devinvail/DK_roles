About

The SAMHSA Behavioral Health Disaster Response App was developed by the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) for use by behavioral health emergency responders. SAMHSA's mission is to reduce the impact of substance abuse and mental illness on America's communities. Visit samhsa.gov for more information. For publications and online products, visit store.samhsa.gov.

The SAMHSA Behavioral Health Disaster Response App received a Silver Web Health Award in the mobile category, sponsored by the National Health Information Center!
