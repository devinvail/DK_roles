Offline Mode

While offline, the app will provide limited functionality:

  • In the Treatment Locator You will not be able to conduct new searches or view the map until the device is back online; however you will be able to load all the facility data from any of your previously saved searches;
  • External Resources will not be load properly; only PDF files which were previouly downloaded through the app will be accesible offline;
  • You are not guaranteed to have the latest article and facility data; make sure to close and reopen the app when back online to ensure the latest data has been received;
